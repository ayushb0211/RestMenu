Balaji Pure Veg

Akurdi, Pune

Balaji Pure Veg is a fast food and quick service restaurant located in the heart of the city. It offers a wide variety of delicious and nutritious vegetarian dishes. The menu includes North Indian, South Indian, Chinese and Continental cuisines. The restaurant has a pleasant and cozy ambience with comfortable seating arrangements. The staff is friendly and attentive. The food is freshly prepared and served hot. The prices are reasonable and the quality of food is excellent. Balaji Pure Veg is the perfect place to enjoy a delicious and healthy meal with family and friends.
